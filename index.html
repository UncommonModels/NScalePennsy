Welcome to the N Scale Pennsy project

This site chronicles my attempt to be prototypically correct to the Pennsylvania Railroad's operations in the "Fleet of Modernism" era (1938~1948). I attempt to list (mostly) accurate product offerings for each piece of rolling stock on the market today. Occasionally I may list "close enough" substitutions for hard to find items. In the long term, this will help me facilitate market research for car or locomotive kits that may be released under the Uncommon Models banner.

Got questions, comments, or suggestions? Email me at nscalepennsy@uncommonmodels.com
